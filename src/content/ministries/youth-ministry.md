## Our Mission

To empower teenagers and young adults to live fully for Christ by providing relevant teaching, strong small groups, and opportunities to serve and lead.

## What We Do

### Weekly Youth Gatherings
- Worship and practical Bible teaching
- Small groups for discussion and life application
- High-energy games

### Discipleship & Mentoring
- One-2-one Group with Youth and youth leaders
- Leadership development tracks for student leaders

## Getting Involved

We welcome youth from ages 13-25. Volunteers and leaders must be committed to regular attendance and to the discipleship process.

Contact Ludwig Eksteen for information about serving or joining a youth group.
